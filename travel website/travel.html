why Kitulgala

why choose Kitulgala?

A walk into the rain forest: Around 4 hours walking through a very dense rainforest with beautiful and hidden waterfalls. Take the suspended bridge and follow the road. There will be a school playground, you cross it straight. Then you will go through a small village and after a little more walk, you will arrive to the office where you can pay the fee for the entrance. Ask local people because there is no sign to indicate where the rainforest is. Also be careful, there are leeches in this forest, so sneakers and socks will be good partners...
